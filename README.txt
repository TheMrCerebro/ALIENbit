[*] Introduction:
=============

 A human has been captured by an evil alien just to play with him
 before eating him. Survive everything you need to escape this
 horrible nightmare. You will have to take all the possible coins
 in case you escape alive and you will be a millionaire
 (you never escape, but you don't know it xD).


=========
[*] Controls:
=========

 Left/Right - Move the player.
 X - Accept/Jump.
 F4 - Full screen/Window mode.
